## Demo App
A personal wellness companion built using Streamlit that helps you track your mental health, express your thoughts, and chat with a supportive AI friend.

✨ Features
🌤 Daily Mood Tracker – Select your mood and log your feelings.

📓 Journal Entry – Write freely about your day and save private reflections.

🤖 AI-Powered Chatbot – Chat with “MindMate,” a supportive mental health assistant powered by GPT-3.5.

📊 Mood Trends Visualization – See your emotional patterns over time in a chart.

🌈 Mood-Based Quotes – Get inspirational messages tailored to your current feelings.

☁️ Word Cloud of Thoughts – Visualize your most-used words from past journal entries.
